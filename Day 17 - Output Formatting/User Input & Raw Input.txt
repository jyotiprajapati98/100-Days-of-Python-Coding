input(prompt): When programar wants to take input from the users then they can use the input().
Example:
val =  input("enter your salary: ") # we can add \n here for line break
print(val)

OUTPUT:
enter your salary: 1500000
1500000

Typecasting in Python:
a) int(input("write the statement to take input"))
Example:
n = int(input("enter salary: "))
print(n)
print(type(n))

m = float(input("enter salary: "))
print(m)
print(type(m))

OUTPUT:
enter salary: 1500000
1500000
<class 'int'>
enter salary: 1500000
1500000.0
<class 'float'>
